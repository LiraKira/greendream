Waste sorting is obligatory in Croatia.
Cans for recycling (plastic, metal, paper and glass) are outside, under the stairs, near the gate/entrance.

The rest of useless garbage from kitchen and bathrooms may only be disposed of in standardized waste blue “ZG bags” (under the sink).
That goes into the black garbage can that is located upstairs at the street, near the other entrance to the house.

There are cleaning supplies and the washing machine in our central heating room (near the yellow bedroom).
For the washing machine you need to open a water valve and plug in electricity.

It is prohibited to touch the boiler or to allow children in the boiler room.
You can also lock it.

The pool is open for use from 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m.

Please respect the Security Warnings exposed at the swimming pool such as:

  • All guests must shower before using a pool

  • Children under the age of 4 require water proof diapers in the pool

  •  No jumping

  • ...
